4. After Submission

4.1 Review Process

Your application will go through a two-stage approval process:

  1. Committee Review: Initial review by internship committee members
  2. Coordinator Approval: Final approval by internship coordinator

Timeline: Review typically takes 5-7 business days

4.2 Application Statuses

Status Description Your Action
‚è≥ Pending Under review by committee or coordinator Wait for decision; monitor your email
‚úÖ Approved Both committee and coordinator approved Accept or decline the placement
‚ùå Rejected Committee or coordinator rejected Review remarks and reapply with corrections

4.3 Accepting Your Placement

Once both committee and coordinator approve your application:

  1. You will receive an email notification
  2. Log in to InternLink and view your application
  3. Click "Accept" to confirm the placement
  4. You can only accept ONE placement
‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes:
  • You can only have one accepted placement at a time
  • Once you accept, you cannot apply for other placements unless you request a change
  • Declining a placement allows you to apply for other opportunities

5. Editing and Managing Applications

5.1 When You Can Edit

You can edit your application ONLY when:

  • Both committee and coordinator status are "Pending"
  • No review has been made yet

5.2 After Approval

Once approved, you cannot edit the application. If you need to change your placement:

  1. Click "Request Change" on your accepted application
  2. Provide a detailed justification (minimum 20 characters)
  3. Upload supporting documents
  4. Wait for committee and coordinator approval
  5. If approved, you can then submit a new application

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I apply for multiple companies?

A: Yes, you can submit multiple applications. However, you can only accept ONE placement.

Q: What if my application is rejected?

A: Review the lecturer's remarks carefully, make necessary corrections, and submit a new application.

Q: Can I change my placement after accepting?

A: Yes, but you must submit a change request with valid justification. If approved, you can then apply for a new placement.

Q: How long does the approval process take?

A: Typically 5-7 business days, but it may vary depending on committee and coordinator availability.

Q: Can I edit my application after submission?

A: Yes, but only while both statuses are still "Pending". Once reviewed, you cannot edit.

Q: What if I don't have an allowance?

A: That's fine. The allowance field is optional and doesn't affect approval.
